About Us

  1. About Our Product

    We are not affiliated with any of the brands whose designs inspire us. All our products are replicas created in the style of popular models. We aim to make the luxury look accessible to everyone.

  2. Payment

    We work on a full prepayment basis, but partial payment is also possible — please check the conditions with our manager. This is because our assortment includes a wide variety of models, colors, and sizes, and our financial capacity for stocking large volumes is limited.

    In some cases, specific items may be available with cash on delivery — such products are posted separately in our Telegram channel.

    At the moment, all orders are accepted exclusively via Telegram or in Instagram Direct.

  3. Delivery

    Free worldwide shipping.

    Delivery to a Nova Poshta branch in Ukraine — from 70 UAH.

    Order processing takes from 12 to 14 business days.

  4. Custom Orders

    We accept orders for shoes and other clothing even if they're not currently in stock. You can choose exactly what you want and receive it in the shortest possible time.

  5. Referral Program and Discounts

    We have a referral system, discounts, and other bonuses. If you have a promo code, be sure to inform the manager during your purchase — the discount will be applied to your order.

    After each order, you'll also receive a thank-you letter with a personal promo code for a 10% discount on your next purchase.

    You can also invite a friend: if they place an order, you’ll receive a 5% discount on any pair.
